## About


I am a **Ph.D.-trained biomedical engineer** working at the intersection of **molecular diagnostics, digital microfluidic platforms, and data-driven laboratory informatics**.


My current research focus spans:
- **Translational molecular diagnostics**: qPCR, LAMP, and CRISPR-based nucleic acid detection systems
- **Digital microfluidic sample-to-answer platforms**: automated nucleic acid extraction, amplification, and detection
- **Biomarker-driven assay design**: from biological hypothesis to validated diagnostic workflow
- **Laboratory informatics & structured data workflows**: traceability, reproducibility, and decision-support systems
- **Cross-functional R&D coordination**: connecting biology, engineering, data science, and operations teams


I am particularly drawn to roles that integrate **disease biology, experimental platforms, quantitative workflows, and translational decision-making** in structured product-oriented research environments.
